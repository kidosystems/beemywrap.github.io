Fantastic Ways to Use Your Beeswax Wrap

Are you trying to be more Eco-friendly, save the environment, and cut costs? Yes, using the beeswax wraps is one way to give the environment a new lease of life. It is a superb way to cut down on waste in the kitchen, by replacing non-degradable plastic wraps with beeswax wraps. Although the beeswax wraps are not new inventions, we've found new and amazing ways to use them, and the best part is, they keep your plastic use way lower than you would expect.

Here are smart ways to use the beeswax wraps, consider this, and you would be eco-friendly in style

  • Make your beeswax wraps into a box to save fresh berries and grapes – Yes! Your beeswax wraps can be molded into a box to keep your fruits. This would not only keep them fresh but will secure them. Just make sure to pat them dry before storage.
  • Make your beeswax wraps into a folded pocket to store your snacks – These amazing beeswax wraps can be converted into pouches and pockets for you to store your treats. It proves to be environmentally friendly as well as affordable.

  • Need an alternative to a straw? Yes! The beeswax wraps can do this. You want an alternative to the plastic or paper straws; the beeswax wraps are up to the task. Although it needs a little mastery to get it right, once you get the hang of it, you will never go wrong. The best part is, you can unwrap it and wash and wrap it back with ease.
  • Refrigerator lining – Beeswax wraps can be used as a liner for refrigerators. You can put this in place to ease the cleaning of the space. Beeswax wraps are perfect for this purpose; they are sticky and adhere to surfaces with ease. You peel it away, wash the liner, and place it back.

  • Wrap your soap for longevity – Want to keep your soap lasting longer, especially when travelling. It is less messy than when a plastic wrap is used, and it is also breathable. The best part of using this to store your soap is it does not take space and would not ruin your clothes if you are packing for a trip.
  • A foldable bowl – When you are on the go, and you need a bowl, you can pinch in a whim; the beeswax wraps make great makeshift bowls. You need a water vessel while on a walk, it's perfect! You need a food bowl for brunch; you can have it in your pocket.

Here you go! These are six ways to use the beeswax wraps, be eco-friendly and creative. Please let us know other creative ways to use the beeswax wraps.
